About Me

My name is Malorie Alyssa Lara. I am 22 years old and enjoy many things, but particularly, spending time at home or with friends. I am a home-body person but when I'm out with friends, I like to be active and social. My family consists of my mom, Rebecca, dad, Jose, sister, Hailea, and two little dogs, Eloise and Hazel.

Throughout my life I have played many sports, but since I was 9, I have loved soccer the most. When I was 10, I had a spiral fracture that you would think would have kept me from playing, but it didn't. It actually made me that much more excited and tough for the upcoming seasons.

When it came to college, I wanted to be close enough to home but also far enough to where I was able to become my own person and do my own things. I chose a school that was about an hour away. That school was Southwestern University. I was able to fulfill my dreams and play soccer for the university at the collegiate level. I was also able to check off a goal when I scored my first collegiate hat-trick my sophomore year.

Some hobbies I enjoy are: Soccer/Sports, Kayaking, Paddleboarding, Swimming, Relaxing and sipping on Margs or a beer with my friends.
